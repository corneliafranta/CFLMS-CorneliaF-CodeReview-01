My favorite cake

This Carrot cake is such a treat; easy to prepare, super delicious, and even vegan. If you quickly need a cake that everyone will love i recomand you to try this all time classic. Together with the creamy lemon frosting it's just perfect for every occasion! Its so rich in flavor and because carrots are already sweet this cake doesnt require a lot of sugar for it to taste great.

Summer is calling

For warm days in late spring i really recommend you to try out this refreshing beautiful tarte. Everyone of the three layers adds something to the cake and combined they make out of a simple tarte a piece of art. On the bottom there is a sweet and tangy strawberry rhubarb layer. To add a nice contrast to the sourness of the first layer i choose a sweet and creamy coconut vanilla cream. And on top is a layer of delicately cut strawberries and some pieces of pistachio to add freshness as well as a nice crunch.

Creamy and delicious

What would the world be without nice creamy chocolate cakes. So delicious and also perfect for every occasion. The cake is fluffy and rich in flavor with a slight hint of coconut. Some coffee helps to bring the chocolate notes. Together with a nice rich chocolate cream, this cake is a dream for every chocolate lover. The cranberrys on the top add a nice contrast lookwise as well as taste wise. It might not look like it but this cake doesnt need either eggs nor milk or butter to be perfect!
